"Explore Your Future Pathways After 12th"

1. Engineering (B.Tech / B.E.)

Duration: 4 years

Description: A degree focusing on various branches of engineering like Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Computer Science, Electronics, and more.

Future Opportunities: Multinational companies, research & development, M.Tech, MBA.

2. Architecture (B.Arch)

Duration: 5 years

Description: A professional degree in designing and planning buildings and structures. Covers urban design, environmental planning, and architectural theory.

Future Opportunities: Architect, urban planner, interior designer, construction management.

3. Pure Science (B.Sc.)

Duration: 3 years

Description: Specializing in subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. This course is ideal for those interested in research, teaching, or higher education.

Future Opportunities: Research, teaching, M.Sc., or higher studies.

4. Defense Services (NDA)

Duration: 3 years (training at NDA) + 1 year (at IMA/IMA/OTA)

Description: A rigorous training program for students interested in joining the Indian Army, Navy, or Air Force as officers.

Future Opportunities: Indian Army, Navy, Air Force officer.

5. Computer Applications (BCA)

Duration: 3 years

Description: A specialized undergraduate degree focusing on computer programming, software engineering, and IT systems.

Future Opportunities: IT services, software development, system analysis, tech companies.

1. MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine and Surgery)

Duration: 5.5 years (including 1 year of internship)

Description: A professional degree in medicine, focusing on diagnosing and treating illnesses, conducting surgeries, and patient care.

Future Opportunities: Doctor, specialist, hospital work, medical research.

2. BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery)

Duration: 5 years

Description: A degree focusing on dental care, including surgeries, fillings, and overall oral health management.

Future Opportunities: Dentist, private practice, dental hospitals, government services.

3. BAMS (Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery)

Duration: 5.5 years

Description: A degree in Ayurveda, focusing on traditional healing methods with herbs, yoga, and diet.

Future Opportunities: Ayurvedic doctor, holistic wellness centers, alternative medicine.

4. BHMS (Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery)

Duration: 5.5 years

Description: A degree focusing on homeopathic medicine to treat various ailments using natural remedies.

Future Opportunities: Homeopathic practitioner, research, clinics, pharmaceutical industry.

5. B.Sc. Nursing

Duration: 4 years

Description: A degree for training nurses in various healthcare settings, focusing on patient care, hospital management, and healthcare systems.

Future Opportunities: Nurse, hospital administration, healthcare sector, NGOs.

6. Pharmacy (B.Pharm)

Duration: 4 years

Description: A degree focusing on drug formulation, pharmacy laws, and patient health management related to medications.

Future Opportunities: Pharmacist, pharmaceutical companies, drug regulatory services.

7. BPT (Bachelor of Physiotherapy)

Duration: 4 years

Description: Training in physical therapy to help patients recover from physical injuries or surgeries.

Future Opportunities: Physiotherapist, rehabilitation centers, hospitals.
